About

Hey there. My name is Emma. I'm a designer who loves creating amazing user experiences.

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Design from Texas A&M University - Corpus Christi, and I recently completed my Master's Degree in Web Design through the University of Florida.

My process is about more than making websites look good. It's about providing valuable content that's accessible to your users. Valuable content can range from the written word to a physical product. I get to know your users before I even put a pencil to paper. Yeah I can code, but I'm a lot more interested in solving your user's problems first and foremost. Check out my work.
